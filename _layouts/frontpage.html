Visual and Intelligent Learning Lab

VILL is affiliated with the Key Laboratory of Embedded System and Service Computing and the Laboratory of Intelligent Information Processing at Tongji University. It is located in the Zhixin Building at Tongji University's Jiading Campus in rooms 476, 431, and 501. The lab is led by Professor Cairong Zhao from Tongji University. VILL's primary research interests encompass computer vision, machine learning, data mining, pattern recognition, and related fields. The current main research areas include topics like face recognition, re-identification of pedestrians, pedestrian tracking, and the study of uncertainty in deep learning theory.
